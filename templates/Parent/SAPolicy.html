S.A. Policy

WHAT IS S.A. (STANDARDISED ASSESSMENT) ?

The applicants who successfully submit their application form, are intimated by the admission officer with the date, time and location for the S.A. (Standardised Assessment). A base line assessment which helps the teachers understand the child’s areas of improvement.

The child is tested in two subjects:

  1. Language (English & Hindi)
  2. Mathematics

Languages: In primary section, the student is tested for his/her reading, understanding, and writing skills along with grammar which he/she would have learnt in previous classes. In secondary section Comprehension and content writing skills are also added. All these skills are necessary for grooming the child in language skills.

Mathematics: In Mathematics the student is tested for concepts and their application. In primary section, the child is tested for the operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division while in secondary section student is tested for applying formulas, finding areas, volumes, distance, speed etc. in accordance with their grade curriculum.

After corrections, the teacher’s remarks are written and the areas of improvement are discussed with the parents. All the records are submitted to academic coordinator and admissions officer of the school for further process.

If your results are satisfactory, and the confirmation is received from the academic coordinator, you will be intimidated for the next step of admission.
