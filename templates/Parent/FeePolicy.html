Fee Policy

TERMS & CONDITIONS:

  1. The terms and conditions of school fees policy about fees structure and is subject to change by prevailing law from time to time.
  2. Full-year payment of fees at once and other dues can be paid through Debit and Credit Card/ Demand Draft in the school A/c in favor of “AryaGurukul”.
  3. All fees and other dues to the school can be paid by Debit/Credit Card (2% gateway charges) or Net banking (₹35 charges applicable) through My Lycee mobile application/portal or by Demand Draft in the favor of “AryaGurukul.”
  4. Currently, fees are not accepted in Cash/NEFT/RTGS/IMPS/Cheques either bearer or post dated/Wallets/UPI etc.
  5. Modes of payments like NEFT/RTGS/IMPS/Wallets/UPI will be available shortly.
  6. The school is not obliged to send reminder, written or by any mode for due payment of fees.
  7. If fee payment is made after the due date, a late fee payment of ₹500/- for every 15 days will be charged.
  8. In case of one month defaults in fees payment of due date, the school reserves its rights to take all lawful actions, including removal of student’s name form school register by giving 15 days notice of such action, and failure of compliance of notice the parent shall be responsible for the consequences.
  9. In case of absentees on the re-opening days an amount equivalent to re- admission fee (equal to one month fee) will be charged.
  10. In case of parents who have persistently defaulted in paying fees/dues for previous year/years shall not be eligible for applying for conversion.
