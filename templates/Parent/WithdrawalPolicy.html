Withdrawal Policy

  • All withdrawals from the school can be made only on a written request for the same by the parent/guardian who has signed the application for admission on the prescribed form available with the school office.
  • One clear month’s notice in writing, on the prescribed withdrawal form or one quarter fee in lieu thereof, is required for the withdrawal of the child from school after last working day of February.
  • Transfer Certificate/Leaving Certificate will be issued only when all dues are settled.
  • In case of mid-quarter withdrawals, without prior notice as per the prescribed date parents are liable to pay the entire fees of the quarter commenced.
  • A charge of ₹150 is applicable for duplicate copy of the leaving Certificate or the date of birth or any other extraction from the General Register.

THE PRINCIPAL RESERVES THE RIGHT TO:

  • Suspend attendance
  • Promote or detain pupils
  • Demand withdrawal of any pupil who breaks school rules.
  • Take disciplinary action whenever required to ensure smooth functioning of the school.
